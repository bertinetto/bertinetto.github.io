About

I am currently leading computer vision at Exscientia -- where we apply machine learning to drug discovery and precision oncology. Previously, I spent four years at the autonomous driving startup Five (acquired by Bosch), after having completed my PhD at the University of Oxford with the thesis Learning (to learn) from few examples.
